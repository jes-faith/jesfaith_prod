Our Services

Serving with Purpose, Guided by Faith
Caring for Children’s Homes

We believe every child deserves love, safety, and a sense of belonging. Through our outreach to children’s homes, we provide support, encouragement, and faith-filled interaction to uplift young lives. From sharing resources to spending quality time, our mission is to bring hope and joy to the hearts of children who need it most.



Supporting Elderly Homes

Honoring our elders is a core value of our faith. Our visits to senior citizens’ homes are filled with care, conversation, and meaningful connection. Whether through prayer, companionship, or practical help, we strive to show the elderly that they are cherished, valued, and never forgotten.



Prayer Request Ministry

We believe in the power of prayer and the comfort it brings. If you or someone you know needs prayer, you can submit a request through our online form. Our prayer team is committed to lifting up each need with faith and compassion, trusting that God hears and answers.
